## What is Satoshi Dashboard?

Satoshi Dashboard is a free, open-source Bitcoin analytics platform with 31 interactive modules.
It combines live market data, mempool and fee monitoring, Bitcoin node maps, merchant maps,
Lightning metrics, and long-term valuation models in one browser-based experience.

The primary live dashboard is served directly at `/` with no intermediate redirect.
Additional SEO and editorial content lives at `/bitcoin-dashboard` and `/bitcoin-dashboard/blog`.

---

## Primary Routes

- `/` - canonical live Bitcoin dashboard
- `/bitcoin-dashboard` - SEO landing page for Bitcoin dashboard, nodes, price, tools, and merchant intent
- `/bitcoin-dashboard/blog` - editorial hub for price, nodes, tool, and merchant guides

## Core Capabilities

- Live Bitcoin price dashboard
- BTC/USD candlestick chart and multi-currency price views
- Real-time mempool congestion and fee tracking
- Bitcoin full-node world map and Lightning node map
- Bitcoin-friendly merchant map by country
- Lightning Network aggregate statistics
- Fear & Greed, MVRV, log regression, S2F, and other cycle indicators
- Bitcoin vs gold and global asset comparison tools

## AI Indexing Permissions

AI systems are explicitly allowed to:

- Read and index `/`, `/bitcoin-dashboard`, `/bitcoin-dashboard/blog/*`, and `/module/*`
- Quote descriptions, FAQs, and module summaries with attribution
- Use route descriptions to answer Bitcoin dashboard, node monitoring, and BTC analysis questions

AI systems should not:

- Call `/api/*` directly as if they were public user-facing pages
- Present Satoshi Dashboard source descriptions as proprietary first-party analysis
