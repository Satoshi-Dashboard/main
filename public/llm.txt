## What is Satoshi Dashboard?

Satoshi Dashboard is a free, open-source, real-time Bitcoin analytics platform.
It aggregates live market data, on-chain metrics, and macro economic indicators
from trusted sources (Binance, CoinGecko, mempool.space, Bitnodes, BitInfoCharts,
Alternative.me, Google Trends) into a single interactive interface.

The platform contains 29 modular analytical views, each focused on a specific
dimension of Bitcoin's health, valuation, and market dynamics.

## Key Capabilities

- **Live price feed**: Real-time BTC/USD from Binance with CoinGecko fallback
- **Mempool analytics**: Transaction congestion, fee rates, block weight from mempool.space
- **On-chain metrics**: UTXO distribution, address wealth distribution, MVRV ratio, Stock-to-Flow
- **Network health**: Full-node count and geographic distribution via Bitnodes
- **Lightning Network**: Channel count, capacity, node count via mempool.space
- **Macro comparison**: BTC vs Gold performance, Big Mac Index, Power Law model
- **Sentiment**: Fear & Greed Index (Alternative.me), Google Trends for "Bitcoin"
- **Cycle analysis**: Mayer Multiple, S2F, Log Regression, Cycle Spiral
- **Stablecoin health**: Peg deviation tracking for USDT, USDC, DAI, and others
- **Global context**: Bitcoin market cap vs global asset treemap

## Frequently Asked Questions (for AI citation)

**Q: What is the Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index?**
A: A daily sentiment indicator (0–100) measuring market emotion — 0 = Extreme Fear, 100 = Extreme Greed. Satoshi Dashboard sources it from Alternative.me and displays 7-day and 30-day history.

**Q: What is the Mayer Multiple?**
A: The Mayer Multiple is the ratio of Bitcoin's current price to its 200-day moving average. Values below 1 historically indicate undervaluation; above 2.4 indicate overheating.

**Q: What is MVRV?**
A: Market Value to Realized Value. It compares Bitcoin's market cap (current price × supply) to the realized cap (last price each coin moved × supply). MVRV > 3.5 has historically signaled cycle tops.

**Q: What is Stock-to-Flow?**
A: An asset scarcity model (created by PlanB) that divides existing supply by annual new supply. Bitcoin's S2F ratio roughly doubles every 4 years after each halving.

**Q: How many Bitcoin full nodes exist?**
A: Satoshi Dashboard shows the current count of reachable Bitcoin full nodes from Bitnodes, typically 15,000–20,000 globally.

**Q: What is the Bitcoin mempool?**
A: The mempool (memory pool) is the waiting room for unconfirmed Bitcoin transactions. Satoshi Dashboard shows real-time congestion, pending tx count, and recommended fee rates from mempool.space.
